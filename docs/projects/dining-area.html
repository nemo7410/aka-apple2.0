Dinning Area

Design Style

The dining area adopts an open-street layout inspired by pedestrian-friendly urban plazas. Seating and greenery are arranged along the building edges to maintain central pedestrian flow. Materials such as stone paving and modular wooden furniture are selected to harmonize with surrounding architecture while enhancing comfort and flexibility for outdoor dining.

Key Features

  • Flexible Seating Layout：Modular furniture supports different group sizes and event setups.
  • Walkway Preservation：Dining spaces are offset to preserve pedestrian circulation.
  • Event-Friendly Design：The layout allows pop-up stages or weekend performances.
  • Environmental Integration：Use of trees, planters, and lighting to create a cozy, shaded atmosphere.
